How to play

Reversi (also called Othello) is played on a square grid, starting with two black and two white discs in the centre.

  • Black moves first. Players alternate turns placing one disc per turn.
  • A move must capture. You can only place a disc where it brackets one or more of the opponent's discs in a straight line (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal), with your own disc at the other end.
  • Flip captured discs. All opponent discs bracketed by your move are flipped to your colour.
  • Valid moves are highlighted. Small dots mark every square you can legally play.
  • Pass. If you have no valid move, your turn is skipped.
  • Game ends when neither player has a valid move. The player with the most discs wins.
